Letters in bubble-style look like they have been blown up with air like balloons. All edges are round. This style does not leave plenty of scope for variation and creativity, but can be executed very quickly and thus is good for learning the basic design principles of drawing graffiti.

Lettering Graffiti

1 Draw the outline sketch for the letters with pencil or pen.


2 Add actual lines for the graffiti designs on the letters. Most graffiti designs are connected, intersecting or on top of one part of the other.


3 Fill in the basic colors.


4 Add more details by drawing a design on the graffiti.

M A T E R I A L S

What are spray paint caps?

Spray paint caps, often called nozzles or nibs, connect to the top of paint cans. When squeezed, spray can tops discharge paint via the valve. Caps for spray paint come in numerous forms, sizes, and colours. To artists, they're like paintbrushes. Different caps allow for delicate and broad lines, exactly like paintbrush sizes.

S P R A Y C A P S

Different types of spray paint caps

Caps can be split into 6 different categories depending on the width of the line they produce and/or the different effects they create. The main categories are super skinny caps, skinny caps, medium caps, fat caps, super fat caps, and special caps.
